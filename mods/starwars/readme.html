1: The races

There are two races in the Star wars mod.
These do not equate to good an evil and are built as closely as possible to the two forces known in the starwars films.

1.1: The Empire

One of the bigger forces, and arguably, more powerful force in starwars is the empire.
They use fearce battle droids and sith warriors to decimate there foes in ruthless asults.
This race is shown in this mod.

1.2: The rebbles.

The rebbles, or the republic, are a smaller group of warriors who are a republic from the empire.
When the jedi and sith go to war, so does the empire and the rebbles.
The rebbles use sweeping but yet still devistating asults to slowly crush the empire in its own power.
This race is represented as the rebble alliance in the mod.

2: The units and buildings

Both the rebbles and the empire have building changes until even the developer can't see the similarities to the original game.
These units and buildings, along with there keystrokes, are explained below.

2.1: The empire

Below is a list of the empires units and buildings.

2.1.1: Foot, or soldier units

  • Aliens: build the buildings and exploit the resources. Key E
  • storm trooper. Offensive unit. Key r
  • Sith. Offensive force wielder. Key R
  • Sith Lord. Offensive force wielder. Key R

2.1.2: Moving, or tank units

  • Armoured asult tank. Offensive tank. Key T
  • Corprol alliance tank droid. Droid/tank. Key T
  • Dwarf spider droid. Powerful droid. Key u
  • Hell fire droid. Renamed from hail fire droid, powerful droid. Key o
  • Multi troop transport. Transport ship. Key p
  • Walker. Extremely powerful one man walking tanks. Key p.

2.1.3: Air or flying units

  • Star destroyer. Modifyed from space unit to flying air unit. Key p
  • Star cruiser. Modifyed from space unit to flying air unit. Key p
  • Droid gun ship. Droid/air ship. Key Y
  • Tie fighter. Modifyed from space unit to flying air unit. Key P
  • Death Star. Extremely powerful moving battle station. Modifyed from space unit to flying air unit. Key p.

Buildings

The standard SoundRTS keys can be used for the buildings.
  • Factories. Recruits aliens. upgrades to great supplies factory and great metals factory to give boosts in resources.
    Note:When upgraded, the factories can not recruit aliens.
  • Farm. Food boost
  • Library. Place of research.
  • Troop factory. Recruits storm troopers.
  • Tank factory. Recruits armoured asult tanks
  • droid factory. Recruits droids [CATD's, droid gun ships, etc.]
  • Transport factory. Recruits multi troop transport.
  • Walker factory. Recruits Walkers.
  • Air factory. Recruits all air units not recruited in other buildings.
  • Sith temple. Recruits sith and sith lord
  • Death star factory. Recruits the death star!

3: Research

There are several pieces of research you can do.

3.1: Empire

  • Duplification. Required to build great supplies factory and great metals factory

3.2: Rebbles.

  • Duplification. Required to build great supplies factory and great metals factory
  • Self propelled heavy artillery research. Enables you to build Self propelled heavy artillery.

6: installation

If you installed the .exe version, you may skip over this part.
This is for the zip file download.
  1. Unzip the folder.
  2. Go to the mod folder, press enter on it. You should be in a folder that contains a folder call starwars mod and a file.
  3. Copy the starwars mod folder into your local soundRTS mods folder.
  4. Go to your local SoundRTS users folder and press enter on it, open soundRTS.ini file.
  5. Search for the following lines with out the quotes "mods =" Delete it.
  7. Copy the following line of text exactly. mods = starwarsmodv1.0
  8. Paste in the line above.
  9. Save the file. Open your SoundRTS game and start a new game. You should now be running the starwars mod version 1.0!
  10. Enjoy!
