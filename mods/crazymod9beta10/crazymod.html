1. Preliminary Notes

  • for more general information about the Sound RTS Crazy Mod and download links, go to http://pragmapragma.free.fr/soundrts-crazymod/en/
  • the latest version of this documentation can always be found at https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/2138237/sound%20rts%20crazy%20mod/english.html
  • unless stated otherwise, buildings or units are repairable/healable.
  • the total resource cost and total time cost are cumulative. For example, the total cost of a mainframe computer is 384 gold, the cost of upgrading a mini computer to a mainframe computer would be 384 - 128 = 256 gold. The same applies for time costs.
  • The upgrade of a building or unit inherits all properties from the base building/unit, unless stated otherwise. For example, barracks are prerequisites of keeps and hence castles. For brevity, most of these inherited properties will not be restated for the upgrade unless it is important.
  • I'm fairly certain that harm or heal levels are multiples of 0.13. For example, harm level 2 does 0.26 damage per second.
  • Buildings and units that are able to detect invisible enemies can only do so for their current square, and not for adjacent squares (if they can see into them)
  • Unless stated otherwise, units have a transport volume of 1
  • as of version 8.3, defeat occurs as long as there are no remaining units and buildings that requires meadows. That means that if you only have buildings such as towers and traps, you will be defeated.

2. Dark Race

This race can overwhelm enemies in numbers, thanks to its low-cost units. This is especially true with the use of necromancy to create undead units from the corpses of fallen enemies. This fact, combined with deadly spells that affect healable units and a flying invisible unit which detects invisibility itself will worry the toughest opponents!

Research

This race does not possess research of its own. However, the following units can benefit from research, which must be obtained either by converting enemy buildings or through an ally that can perform the necessary research.

  • undead slaves will experience improved wood gathering after the construction of a sawmill
  • Ghouls can benefit from the blacksmith's melee armor upgrade,. Spectrum in Armor and zombies benefit from both melee armor and weapons upgrades
  • Skeletons benefit from the sawmill's archer's armor, weapon and range upgrades.
  • Bone dragons benefit from all dragon's lair research

Special Abilities

  • Call ectoplasm: the spectral tower can recruit ectoplasm which lasts for 2.5 minutes on the current square. Costs 150 mana.
  • Bewitchment: ectoplasm can bewitch an enemy building or unit in the current square, causing it to switch to your team. Costs 75 mana.
  • Evil fog: the spectral tower can summon evil fog lasting 90 seconds on the current or adjacent squares. The fog does damage to all enemy and friendly healable units at harm level 2. It also turns friendly buildings and units on the current square invisible for its effect duration. Costs 200 mana.
  • Call spook lights: the lich can summon 2 spook lights on the current square which last for 2 minutes. Costs 125 mana.
  • Deadly fog: the lich can summon deadly fog on the current or adjacent squares which lasts for a minute. The fog does damage at harm level 1 to all enemy and friendly healable units on the square. Costs 150 mana.
  • Pass through walls: the lich can teleport itself and any friendly units in the square to an adjacent square. Costs 200 mana.
  • Raise dead: necromancers can use corpses on the current square to create undead minions lasting 10 minutes. Each casting of the spell creates 3 zombies, 3 spectrum in armor, 3 phantoms, 2 flesh golems and a bone dragon. Fewer or no minions will be summoned if there are not enough corpses available. Costs 75 mana.
  • Sew darkness tree: summons a tree of darkness lasting 1000 seconds on the current square. The tree can attack airborn units and create carnivourous plants from corpses on the square. Costs 175 mana.
  • Death's breath: the bone dragon can use its deadly breath to do damage at harm level 30 to healable units on the current or adjacent squares for 3 seconds. Costs 200 mana.
  • Recycle dead into carnivourous plants: the darkness tree creates carnivourous plants from corpses on the square. However, some of the corpses can become thorny plants instead. Costs 125 mana.

3. Elementals

Having a mysterious sensitivity permitted by mastering the elements, all units and buildings of this race can detect invisible units. Elementals do not require food. Elemental units also have the highest offensive and defencive stats, being the most powerful race. However, this comes at exorbitantly high gold, wood and construction/recruitment times. It will take some time before elementals can secure a foothold in the map, during which time they will be vulnerable to the attacks of other races. However, once elementals have established themselves, they will be a difficult force to combat.

6. Robots

The large amounts of gold required to develop robotic buildings may deter some - especially since all units require at least a secondary building. However, once the required infrastructure has been developed, robotic units can be recruited at relatively low cost. Additionally, with the exception of geeks, robotic units don't require food. Robotic units are relatively fragile but have excellent offences; robots are the only race with units capable of attacking both ground and airborn targets in adjacent squares. Finally, it should be noted that robots are vulnerable against invisible enemies, as developing the technology to detect invisible units takes some time.

Special Abilities

  • Grand explosion: the explosives magazine can detinate, causing a huge explosion that does damage at harm level 800 for 2 seconds to both airborn and grounded buildings or units in the square. This skill does not differentiate between friend and foe.
  • Small explosion: the hidden bomb can detinate, causing a small explosion that does damage at harm level 60 for 3 seconds to any ground buildings or units in the square. This skill does not differentiate between friend and foe.

Special Abilities

All the special abilities for this race are clerical in nature.

  • Conversion: Causes an enemy building or enemy unit to join the user's team at the cost of 150 mana.
  • Exorcism: does damage to undead at harm level 2 for 1 minute when cast on the current or adjacent squares. Super exorcism research increases the damage dealt. Costs 150 mana.

  • Resurrection: resurrects up to 6 friendly units using their corpses, starting from the oldest corpse, restoring their health to one-third of the maximum. Costs 150 mana.
